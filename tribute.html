J.R.R. Tolkien

The father of high fantasy

Tolkien in 1916. He is wearing a WWI British army uniform.


The life of J.R.R. Tolkien:

         
  • Tolkien was born on January 3rd, 1892.
  • He married his wife, Edith, in 1916. They were married until her death in 1971.      
  • Tolkien served in the British army from 1915 to 1920. He saw action in World War 1.
  • Tolkien's The Hobbit was published in 1937, followed by The Lord of The Rings published in three volumes from 1954-1955
  • Tolkien was a longtime friend of fellow author C.S. Lewis. Lewis' conversion to Christianity was in part due to his relationship with Tolkien.
  • Tolkien died in 1973, aged 81 years.
